You may be wondering

How do I sign up for a free trial? What happens after the free trial period?

To sign up for your free 30 day free trial, please click on “Try Prime free for 30 days” button above. Although you will not be charged for your 30 day trial of Prime, we do need a valid payment option on file in order to start enjoying your trial and Prime's benefits.

What benefits does Prime offer?

Prime offers you the best of entertainment including movies, TV series, Amazon Originals, free PC games and in game content with Prime Gaming, and fast and free delivery.

What methods can I use to sign up for Prime?

To sign up, you must have a valid payment method on file. Payment methods accepted include international and domestic Visa and MasterCard credit and debit cards. Don't have a credit or debit card? Call #266# from your Orange mobile number to subscribe to Amazon Prime and start your free trial

What if I am already a Prime member on a different Amazon website?

If you live in Egypt and have Amazon Prime on another Amazon website, we encourage you to change your subscription to Amazon.eg. Enjoy a wide selection of Egyptian brands with Prime delivery, customer service in Egypt and access to a wide range of movies and series on Prime Video. And all this for EGP 29.00/month or EGP 249.00/year!

If I am not satisfied, how do I cancel? Will I get a refund?

You can cancel your Prime membership at any time by clicking on “Manage my membership” [https://www.amazon.eg/prime]. You will not receive a refund for the months during which your membership was active, but you will not be charged for your next scheduled billing.

What if I already have an Amazon Prime Video subscription?

Prime Video is included in Amazon Prime. If you are already a Prime Video subscriber in Egypt, you can cancel your Prime Video subscription and sign up for Prime on Amazon.eg and get 30 days free trial. Otherwise, you will be automatically migrated at the end of your billing cycle.
